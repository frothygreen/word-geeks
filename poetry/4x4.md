## 4 x 4

structure:
- 4 word poem
- 4 lines
- first and last word are the same

order:
- the 'Host' adds the first (and fourth) word
- the 'Guest' adds the second word (starting arc)
- the 'Host' adds the third word (ending arc)
- the 'Guest' adds the title

<br/>
examples:
<br/><br/>

**WINTER**  

snow  
fall  
falling  
snow

<br/>  

**ASH**  

snow  
fall  
out  
snow 

<br />

**FLOWER**

spring  
loaded  
colour  
spring
